THE BRIEF

INCOGNITO 2026 is Seshadripuram College's premier intercollegiate technical and cultural fest — designed for the bold, the curious, and the relentlessly competitive.

One day. Multiple arenas. Hundreds of agents from colleges across the city converging on a single mission: to solve, build, perform, and dominate.

The theme is crime-scene noir. Expect the unexpected. Decode challenges. Trust nothing. Win everything.
